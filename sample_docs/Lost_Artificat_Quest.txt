Quest Name: The Lost Artifact
Quest Type: Main Story

Objective:
- Retrieve the lost artifact from the Ancient Ruins.
- Return it to the Guild of Mages.

Steps:
1. Talk to Elder Magus to receive quest.
2. Travel to Ancient Ruins.
3. Defeat Guardian Golem.
4. Solve the rune puzzle to unlock artifact chamber.
5. Retrieve artifact and return to Guild.

Rewards:
- Experience Points: 1500
- Gold: 500
- Unique Item: Staff of Elements
- Unlocks: New Mage spells

Notes:
- Player must have at least Level 5 to start quest.
- Puzzle can be skipped if player has Ancient Key item.
