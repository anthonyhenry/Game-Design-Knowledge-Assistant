Level: Castle Siege
Type: Combat / Defense

Overview:
- Players must defend the castle gates from waves of enemies.
- Waves increase in difficulty every 2 minutes.

Enemies:
- Foot Soldiers: Low health, melee attack.
- Archers: Medium health, ranged attack.
- Catapults: Heavy damage, slow movement.

Objectives:
- Survive 10 waves of enemies.
- Protect the King from taking damage.

Environmental Hazards:
- Traps: Spike pits, arrow traps.
- Fireball launchers along castle walls.
- Collapsing bridges on wave 5 and 8.

Power-Ups:
- Healing Potion: Restores 50 health.
- Reinforced Shield: Reduces damage by 25% for 20 seconds.
- Speed Boots: Increases movement by 50% for 15 seconds.
