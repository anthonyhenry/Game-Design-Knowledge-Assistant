Title: Space Raiders
Genre: Sci-Fi Shooter / Strategy
Platform: PC, Console

Gameplay Overview:
Players control a fleet of space ships defending colonies against alien invaders. 
The game mixes real-time strategy with tactical combat.

Player Mechanics:
- Ships have health, shields, and energy bars.
- Energy regenerates at 5 units per second.
- Special abilities: Cloaking, Laser Barrage, Shield Overload.

Enemies:
- Alien Fighters: Weak to lasers, moderate speed.
- Alien Bombers: Slow but high damage.
- Alien Mothership: Boss enemy, requires coordinated fleet attacks.

Power-Ups:
- Shield Boost: Restores 50 shield points.
- Energy Surge: Instantly fills energy bar.
- Targeting Computer: Increases laser accuracy by 25%.
- EMP Pulse: Disables enemy abilities for 5 seconds.

Levels:
- Sector Alpha: Tutorial, easy enemies.
- Sector Beta: Moderate difficulty, introduces Alien Bombers.
- Sector Gamma: Hard mode, Mothership encounter.

Narrative:
The Galactic Council has tasked players with defending colonies from a mysterious alien armada. Player decisions affect faction relationships and unlock alternate endings.
